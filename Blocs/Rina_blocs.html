RINA

Significant Ships of 2018 is a publication by the Royal Institution of Naval Architects (RINA) that showcases some of the most significant ships delivered in 2018. The selected vessels stand out for their innovative designs, advanced technologies, and unique engineering features. The publication also highlights the maritime industry's transition toward greener solutions, including LNG propulsion, exhaust gas cleaning systems (scrubbers), and compliance with the 2020 global sulfur emission regulations. It covers a wide range of ship types, reflecting the latest developments in modern shipbuilding and marine technology.
